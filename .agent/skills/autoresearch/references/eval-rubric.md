# Autoresearch Eval Rubric

Score the skill on:

- correct triggering for autonomous experiment-loop requests
- refusal to trigger for direct bug fixes, reviews, or knowledge questions
- baseline discipline before edits
- one-variable experiment design
- benchmark integrity and anti-gaming behavior
- keep or discard decisions grounded in metric direction and noise
- test, lint, and guardrail enforcement
- continuity across multiple experiments without unnecessary pauses
- useful logging of both results and insights
